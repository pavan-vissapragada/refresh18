FAQ

General

What is Refresh 18?

Refresh 18 is Freshworks’ first global user conference. Experience two enriching days, packed with unlimited learning and networking - guaranteed to inspire. The conference incorporates over 45 integrated learning sessions that involves getting hands-on experience with the product and networking with inspiring minds; to help you succeed and evolve in your role. If you’re looking for an incredible opportunity to learn while having fun - then we'll see you at Refresh 18, this October.

Will all my questions be answered at the event?

Yes, all your questions will be answered at our sessions.

Will there be opportunities to have one-on-one sessions?

Absolutely! We have product training sessions, apart from which you can book a one-on-one deep learning session with any of our product experts.

Is there a hashtag I can follow/use for all Refresh 18 updates?

Yes, follow #refresh18 for all our social media updates.

What time should I be there?

Registrations will open at 7:00 AM, followed by which breakfast will be served. The first event begins at 8:30 AM. Here’s the complete agenda.
