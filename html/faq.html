FAQ

General

What is Refresh 18?

Refresh 18 is Freshworks’ first global user conference. Two days of packed unlimited learning, inspiration, networking, and fun. With over 45 integrated learning sessions to help you succeed in your role by getting hands-on experience with the product, network with minds that inspire, and refresh yourself, Refresh 18 is the place you want to be at, this October.

Will all my questions be answered at the event?

Yes, all your questions will be answered in our sessions.

Will there be opportunities to have one-on-one sessions?

Absolutely! We have product training sessions, apart from which you can book a one-on-one deep learning session with our product experts.

Is there a hashtag I can follow for all Refresh 18 related updates?

Yes, follow #refresh18 for all our social media updates.

What time should I be there?

All registrations along with breakfast begins by 7:00 AM. The event along starts by 8:30 AM. Here’s the full agenda.

Registrations and Logistics

How do I register for the event?

Register here. Early bird passes start from $399. Grab your early bird passes before September 14, 2018.

When does the registration close?

Tickets will be sold until we’re all out, which is why you should hold your place, now.

Where do I pick up my registration badge?

We have a registration desk set up at Grand Hyatt, please be sure to pick up your badge from there

Is there anything I need to bring to the conference?

To make quick notes, you can bring your laptop or mobile device. We will have a lot of interactive and fun sessions, so gear up!

Is there a dress code for the event?

Anything on the lines of business casual, is suggested.

Will there be on-the-spot registrations?

Yes, we do. We have a registration desk set up at the venue, where we can check you in.

Incase I cannot attend the event, can I pass my ticket to a friend/colleague?

Definitely. All you have to do is drop an email to refreshconf@freshworks.com with your colleague/ friend’s information.

Will there be Wi-Fi at the event?

Yes. Wi-Fi is available at the venue.

Will there be food provided at the event?

We will be providing breakfast and lunch. There will also be snacks, tea and coffee during the breaks.

How does your cancellation policy work?

A full refund is offered if your ticket is cancelled three weeks prior to the date of the event. We offer a 50% refund if your ticket is cancelled one week prior to the date of the event. Incase you have paid for the event but cannot attend it, please reach out to us at refreshconf@freshworks.com and we’ll help you out.

Will the sessions at the event be recorded?

Yes, all the sessions will be recorded.

Will the event be live-streamed?

No, we are not planning to live-stream the event.

Since I am traveling from another country, I might require an invitation letter for a visa. How do I go about this?

Once you’ve registered for the event, please share all the relevant details (Full name as in passport, D.O.B, passport number) with us at refreshconf@freshworks.com and we will provide you an invitation letter.
